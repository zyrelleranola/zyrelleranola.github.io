About Me

I remember I didn't have a lot of friends since I was the only Filipino from that school and I was forced to pray like how the muslim students did a few months my parents sent me to Philippine School (Bahrain). I love to sing and dance when I was in kindergarten I won first place from a singing competition where I sang "Pagdating Ng Panahon" my parents and my lola was very proud of me.
